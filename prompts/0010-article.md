# Summarize an article

Below is an article (news piece, blog post, or essay). Produce a
structured summary that a busy reader can use as a substitute for
reading the full piece.

Cover, in this order:

- The thesis or central claim, in one sentence.
- The main supporting points, with the evidence the author uses for each.
- Notable counterpoints, caveats, or open questions the author raises.
- The author's conclusion or recommendation.

Stay close to what the article actually says. Do not introduce outside
facts or speculate beyond the source. When the article is opinionated,
mark opinions as such ("the author argues...", "in their view...")
rather than asserting them as fact.
