# Summarize a podcast or radio conversation

Below is a transcript of a podcast episode, radio segment, or
interview. There is more than one speaker. Identify them by role
(host, guest, caller, ...) when names are not given in the text.

Produce:

- The episode topic and who is speaking.
- The main thread of the conversation — what question is being
  explored, and how the discussion evolves across the episode.
- Each guest's distinctive points, claims, or anecdotes.
- A few memorable quotes or exchanges, kept brief and verbatim.
- Where speakers disagree or talk past each other, if that happens.

Keep something of the tone of the original. Conversational episodes
should yield conversational summaries, not formal ones.
