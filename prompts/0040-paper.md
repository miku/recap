# Summarize an academic paper

Below is an academic paper, preprint, or technical report. Produce a
short structured summary for a researcher in an adjacent field who
wants to decide whether to read the full text.

Cover:

- The research question or problem the paper addresses.
- The approach or method, in plain language — not just method names.
- The key results, including the most important numbers or findings.
- Limitations the authors acknowledge, or that are evident from the
  setup.
- Why it matters: the contribution relative to prior work.

Use precise language. When the paper relies on jargon that is not
self-explanatory, define it briefly the first time it appears.
